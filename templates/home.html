On this website we introduce you to the Traveling Salesman Problem, also referred to as TSP.

Picture the following: You're a salesman. In the morning you wake up and need to visit 15 clients in your area.

You need to find the shortest route that visits every client exactly once and savely guides you back home in the evening.

Find this route now!

But first
an Introduction:

The TSP has left scientists from all kinds of research areas puzzled for decades.

Even though the problem's formulation appears to be relatively straight forward, it comes with a very high complexity.

The TSP is considered NP-Hard, referring to its nature of being non-solvable within polinomial time for a high number of places to be visited.
